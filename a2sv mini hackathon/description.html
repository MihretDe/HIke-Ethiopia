Entoto Mountains

Entoto Mountain is a popular destination near Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, known for its stunning landscapes and cultural significance. The 7.1-km loop trail on Entoto Mountain is a moderately challenging route, taking an average of 1 hour and 47 minutes to complete.
