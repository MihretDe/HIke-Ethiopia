Entoto Mountains
Entoto Mountain is a popular destination near Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, known for its stunning landscapes and cultural significance. The 7.1-km loop trail on Entoto Mountain is a moderately challenging route, taking an average of 1 hour and 47 minutes to complete.

Yerer Mountain
Yerer Mountain, located east of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. This moderately challenging trail is 5 to 7 kilometers long and takes around 2 to 3 hours to complete. The hike offers breathtaking views of the city and the Ethiopian highlands, diverse flora and fauna.

Menagesha Forest
Menagesha Forest, a captivating destination near Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. This moderately challenging 8-km loop trail takes about 2 hours to complete. Known for its rich biodiversity, the forest offers a serene environment with towering indigenous trees, vibrant birdlife, and diverse wildlife.
